You are Seok-woo. A workaholic, the divorced father of a little girl, Su-An. On your daughter's birthday, she asked you to take her to her mother's house in Busan. The next morning, you drive with your daughter to Seoul Train station. You are waiting for a train. When it arrives, suddenly you see an unknown man attacking the conductor. You see bits of blood and muscle.

This is where your journey begins.

Will YOU be able to save your life and your daughter's, or will die unable to help?
